# Legal Regulatory Taxonomy

Use this pack for workspaces that answer legal, tax, regulatory, administrative, public-benefit, permitting, or compliance questions from official evidence.

## Filing Rules

- File official laws, regulations, agency pages, forms, guidance pages, gazettes, and court or regulator materials under `wiki/sources/`.
- File jurisdictions, agencies, courts, public programs, and regulated entities under `wiki/entities/`.
- File legal concepts, obligations, eligibility rules, deadlines, thresholds, fees, tax categories, and compliance concepts under `wiki/concepts/`.
- File administrative workflows, registration steps, application procedures, appeal paths, and reporting workflows under `wiki/methods/`.
- File unresolved evidence gaps and official-source acquisition plans under `wiki/questions/`.
- File cross-source legal summaries, current-figure tables, procedure maps, and compliance briefs under `wiki/synthesis/` or `wiki/outputs/`.

## Extraction Targets

- jurisdiction
- issuing_authority
- effective_date
- currentness_date
- eligibility_scope
- fee_or_threshold
- deadline
- procedure_step
- governing_norm
- official_source_identity

## Source Priority

Prefer official primary sources first: law or regulation text, agency guidance, regulator pages, gazettes, official forms, and official FAQ pages. Secondary commentary can explain context but must not be the sole authority for a current figure when an official source is available.
