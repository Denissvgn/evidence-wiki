# Standards Compliance Claims

Use standards claims only when the claim is backed by local source metadata and
coverage facets.

## Claim Shapes

- `standard_identity`: exact designation, title, body, edition or year, and
  registry URL.
- `standard_currentness`: current status, publication date, replacement chain,
  and retrieved-at metadata.
- `product_requirement`: product category, legal act, harmonised standard,
  OJEU/EUR-Lex reference, and validity signal.
- `register_inclusion`: register entry, category, scope, owner, manager,
  control body, and linked owner references.

## Evidence Rules

Registry metadata can prove identity and status. It does not prove permission to
copy full standards text. Guidance pages can be useful context, but guidance
alone does not satisfy mandatory legal/product facets when OJEU, EUR-Lex, or a
harmonised-standard reference is the controlling authority.
