# Standards Compliance Taxonomy

## Entities

- Standards bodies: ISO, IEC, OGC, NIST, BSI, CEN, CENELEC, ETSI, IETF, IEEE.
- Registries: standards catalogues, open-data registries, harmonised-standard
  lists, OJEU references, GOV.UK registers, NIST publication catalogues.
- Product domains: regulated product families, conformity-assessment scopes,
  geospatial data categories, technical-documentation scopes.

## Concepts

- Standard designation and edition or year.
- Current, withdrawn, superseded, draft, and historical standard status.
- Registry provider, standards body, registry URL, and terms status.
- Product requirement, legal act, OJEU reference, and harmonised-standard link.
- Register owner, register manager, control body, linked owner reference.

## Page Types

- `sources`: registry pages, open-data snapshots, official guidance, legal
  references, and standards-body landing pages.
- `entities`: standards bodies, regulators, commissions, product categories,
  register owners, and control bodies.
- `concepts`: currentness, designation identity, legal authority, conformity,
  register inclusion, and terms limits.
- `claims`: exact standards identity, current-version claims, legal/product
  requirement claims, and register-inclusion claims.
- `synthesis`: standards maps, compliance profiles, current-version tables,
  and evidence-gap reports.
